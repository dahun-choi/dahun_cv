Dahun Choi is a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Information Engineering at the Seoul National University of Science and Technology. He received the M.S. degree in Electrical and Information Engineering from the Seoul National University of Science and Technology, Seoul, Korea, in 2022.

His research interests include efficient deep learning, quantization, and algorithm–hardware co-design for neural networks.
